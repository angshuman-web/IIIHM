Admissions 2026

Your journey into world-class hospitality begins with a single step. Applications are now open.

How to Apply

A Simple, Guided Process

Step 01

Submit Application

Complete the online application form with your details and documents.

Step 02

Counselling & Interaction

A friendly discussion to understand your goals and aspirations.

Step 03

Offer & Scholarship

Receive your admission offer and applicable scholarship details.

Step 04

Enrolment

Confirm your seat, complete formalities and welcome aboard!

Eligibility

Who Can Apply

  • Passed 10+2 in any stream from a recognised board
  • Minimum aggregate as per university norms
  • Passion for hospitality & service
  • Admission based on merit and personal interaction

Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships, early-bird waivers and need-based assistance are available. Details are shared during counselling.
